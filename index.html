余钺

北京大学中文系理论语言学

我的名字是余钺，国际音标为/y35 yɛ51/，你也可以使用我的藏文名 དཔལ་འཛོམས། /hua.ndzom/（音近“花粽”）或英文名 Agnes。
My name is Yu Yue (pronounced /y35 yɛ51/). You are also welcome to call me by my Tibetan name, དཔལ་འཛོམས། (/hua.ndzom/), or my English name, Agnes.

我于2003年冬天出生于湖北，母语为赣语大冶方言和汉语普通话，第一外语为英语，曾短暂学习过安多藏语。
I was born in Hubei in the winter of 2003. I am a native speaker of Mandarin and the Daye Gan Chinese, and English is my first foreign language. I have also spent some time briefly learning Amdo Tibetan.
